# PXL Sales Invoice Functional Specification

Status: Completeness Audit v1
Last updated: 2026-07-15
Reference implementation scope: Sales Invoice create/edit workspace and read-only view workspace
Mandatory field source matrix: `PXL_TRANSACTION_FIELD_SOURCE_MATRIX.md`

## 1. Purpose

The Sales Invoice is the authoritative customer billing transaction for AR recognition, output VAT recognition, collection monitoring, customer ledger drilldown, and future BIR sales reporting.

The Sales Invoice must not display, post, report, or export a business fact unless that fact is:

1. entered by the user,
2. inherited from governed master data,
3. computed by documented rules,
4. generated by authoritative posting/tax services, or
5. clearly identified as not configured or not available.

Never fabricate missing dimensions, cost values, tax details, or document relationships.

The field-level authority for source, storage, editability, appearance, business use, implementation status, and validation status is `PXL_TRANSACTION_FIELD_SOURCE_MATRIX.md`. This specification summarizes the functional behavior; it does not replace the matrix.

## 3A. Separated Commercial and Inventory Accounting Impact

The Sales Invoice workspace presents accounting impact in separate sections for clarity:

- Commercial / Revenue Accounting Impact for customer receivable, revenue, output VAT, discounts, rounding, deferred revenue when supported, and other invoice-side lines.
- Inventory / Cost Accounting Impact for inventory release, COGS, inventory asset reduction, variance/clearing when supported, and related inventory movement traceability.
- Expected Withholding - Informational when expected CWT exists. This section is not part of the Sales Invoice journal unless recognition policy changes.

This is a presentation standard only. The Sales Invoice remains one balanced accounting event. Combined debits and credits must reconcile to the authoritative preview or posted journal. Posted view uses actual immutable journal and inventory movement evidence; draft view uses the server preview when the invoice has been saved.

## 3B. Draft State Management

The Sales Invoice create/edit workspace follows `PXL_TRANSACTION_DRAFT_STATE_STANDARD.md`.

Confirmed root cause of the 2026-07-15 data-loss defect:

- the route initialization effect depended on callback identities that were derived from live draft/reference state;
- while on `/sales-invoices/new`, changing operational dimensions could recreate the initializer and rerun the new-document reset;
- in edit mode, the same pattern could reload saved data over unsaved edits when reference data or initializer callbacks changed.

Corrected behavior:

- one authoritative editable draft object owns header, customer, context, CWT, and line state;
- route initialization is keyed to company, route mode, and invoice id;
- new/edit initialization runs only when the route/document identity changes;
- item, customer, dimension, VAT basis, and line field changes use immutable partial updates;
- preview and lookup data never replace editable draft state;
- header defaults no longer silently overwrite existing line values.

## 6. Posting Summary

Current Sales Invoice posting creates:

| Debit | Credit | Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Accounts Receivable |  | Company AR control account and invoice total |
|  | Revenue | Sales invoice line revenue accounts and net amounts |
|  | Output VAT | Company VAT payable account and invoice VAT amount |
| Cost of Goods Sold |  | Inventory item cost from authoritative valuation |
|  | Inventory | Inventory account from item/line posting source |

Current Sales Invoice posting does not create:

- deferred revenue
- CWT receivable at invoice stage

Expected CWT remains informational until receipt/application/certificate recognition under the AR collection workflow.

## 7. End-to-End Traceability

The required transaction chain is:

Customer -> Sales Order -> Delivery Receipt -> Sales Invoice -> Receipt -> Collection/CWT certificate -> Credit Memo/Return/Reversal

Sales Invoice currently supports the invoice, GL, VAT, receipt application, AR aging, and audit portions of this chain. Sales Order and Delivery Receipt conversion/linkage are not yet complete enough to mark Sales Invoice relationship traceability as fully validated.

## 9. Approval Rule

Sales Invoice implements the global Transaction Workspace architecture but does not define it. It must not be marked business-`VALIDATED` until the open completeness gaps are implemented or explicitly excluded by an approved accounting policy.

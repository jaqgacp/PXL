## Module Overview
The Revenue Recognition Schedules module tracks deferred (unearned) revenue and automates its recognition over time. In Philippine accounting, particularly for service companies, SaaS, or long-term contracts, when a customer pays upfront for a year of service, the amount cannot be immediately recognized as income. It must sit as a Liability (Unearned Revenue) and be gradually moved to Income as the service is delivered. This module manages that schedule, ensuring compliance with PFRS 15 (Revenue from Contracts with Customers).

## Dashboard UI
The interface tracks all upfront billings that require deferred recognition.

### The Action Bar
* **Search Bar:** Search by Customer, Contract Name, or Source Invoice.
* **Filter:** Filter by Status (Active, Completed, Paused).
* **Customize View:** Toggle columns.
* **Export Button:** Export for external audit verification of deferred revenue balances.
* **Create Schedule Button:** Manually create a schedule (usually auto-created from AR Sales Invoices).

### The Data List
* **Schedule ID:** Auto-generated identifier.
* **Customer Name:** Linked to the Contacts module.
* **Description:** E.g., "12-Month Maintenance Contract".
* **Deferred Account:** The GL Liability account.
* **Revenue Account:** The target GL Income account.
* **Total Value:** The total deferred amount.
* **Recognized Amount:** Revenue acknowledged to date.
* **Deferred Balance:** `Total Value - Recognized Amount`.
* **Start Date / End Date:** The performance period.
* **Status:** Active, Completed, Paused.
